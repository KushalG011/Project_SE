How to prepare for campus placements

Improve Your Communication Ability - Communication abilities must be one of the most significant parts of a group conversation. Consistent reading and speaking is the best technique to improve one's communication abilities. Gather a group of friends and do role plays or mock group discussions to prepare.

Be Clear - Before you begin the debate, you should have a clear idea of the topic, how to convey it, and the framework of your argument. Think intelligently before beginning the discussion, and always use a solution-oriented approach rather than constantly discussing simply the problem with no solutions.

Take Command - The whole point of group discussions is to find the best among the crowd; thus, being visible is crucial. It is up to each candidate to steer the discussion and remain relevant throughout. Take cues from what other candidates are saying and incorporate them wherever you see fit. Respond quickly but professionally while respecting everyone's point of view.
